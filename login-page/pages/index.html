How it Works

Our platform simplifies hiring and job search with role-based dashboards, smart resume tools, and AI-powered integrations that keep everyone connected.

1. Easy Onboarding

Sign up as a job seeker, recruiter, or freelancer with quick social login and role-based access.

2. Tailored Dashboards

Each role gets a dedicated dashboard with tools designed for their needs—applications, hiring, or freelancing.

3. Resume & Skill Hub

Resumes are auto-formatted, ATS-scored, and skills extracted to power job matches, career goals, and recruiter filters.
